About Me

Olamilekan is a solution driven developer that believes in technology being the powerful weapon that will rule the world because of its massive impact on humanity in these recent years. I am always eager to use my tech skills to build projects that will provide solution to humanity. I am aspiring to becoming a Full-Stack Software Developer that can bring together the knowledge of both Front-End and Back-End to solve major problems. When I am not coding, I love playing guitar, listening to worship songs and reading tech books.
